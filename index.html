About Me


I am a Ph.D. student at the University of California, Davis working in the Tahoe Environmental Research Center, (TERC) with Ph.D. Geoffrey Schladow, and Ph.D. Fabian Bombardelli, where we are investigating littoral lake hydrodynamics and water quality in lakes by applying 3D numerical modeling. I have a M.S. in Civil and Environmental Engineering from the University of California, Davis, where I worked on boat induced sediment resuspension and water clarity in shallow flows.


My research interests combine numerical modeling and field experiments to better understand the littoral lake hydrodynamics.
